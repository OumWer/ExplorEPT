About the Explor'EPT project

The Explor'EPT project is an innovative initiative aimed at improving the orientation of students interested in the Tunisia Polytechnic School of Tunisia. By providing an interactive platform, it allows students to explore the various fields of study and specificities of the institution, thereby assisting them in making informed decisions regarding their academic and professional orientation. By furnishing precise and detailed information about the different available programs, student life, and organized events, Explor'EPT aims to meet students' informational needs and guide them in their orientation journey. The use of a personalized chatbot also enables interactive responses to students' inquiries, providing them with information tailored to their individual needs. By combining accessibility, accuracy, and personalization, the Explor'EPT project represents a valuable resource for students seeking to explore the opportunities offered by the Tunisia Polytechnic School.
